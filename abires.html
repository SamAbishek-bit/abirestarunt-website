Our Story

A family kitchen, grown into five cities

Abi Restaurant began as one home recipe book — sambar simmered slow, chutneys ground fresh on stone, and a filter coffee ritual nobody was allowed to rush. That same kitchen now feeds five cities, without changing a single ingredient.

Every branch sources vegetables daily, presses its own filter coffee decoction, and follows the same hygiene checklist every morning before the first dosa hits the tawa. Good food, honestly priced, served by people who remember your usual order.
